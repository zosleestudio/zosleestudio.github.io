LL1 (Line Lighting No.1)

keyword: tension, instinctive

field: product, lighting

material: wood, stainless steel, acrylic, LED

size: 60:300:320 (w:l:h)

year: 2013

To express the lighting device as the best product to highlight tension, the designer focused on the characteristic that the stand is basically an indirect product that touches the product only when it is turned on and off, rather than a direct product that is manipulated by the user.

Tension was maximised solely through the proportional composition of the shipping elements on the plane and the seemingly close support.

The user can change the proportion between the components by adjusting the length of the stainless steel rod, at which time the tight connection between the two rods is felt intact. Light is immobile, but the fingertips show that kinetic power is latent.
